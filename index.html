Values

What we stand for

These aren't marketing bullets — they're the decisions we make every day.

🌿

Body positivity

A community that celebrates all bodies without shame or judgment. Moderation enforces this actively.

🔒

Privacy first

No advertising. No third-party data sales. Sensitive content is blurred by default until users opt in.

🤝

Community driven

Feature requests are public and voted on by users via WishKit. The community shapes the roadmap.

✋

Respect for all

Community guidelines are strictly enforced. Harassment, exploitation, and disrespect have zero tolerance.

Boilerplate

For your article

Copy-ready description for publications.

SunSeekers is a free iOS social app for the naturist community, designed to help people connect in real life rather than scroll endlessly online. Built by independent developer Nicolas Rios in California, the app features a social feed, group and direct messaging, stories, and a discovery tool — all with no advertising, no data selling, and strong privacy controls built in from the start. SunSeekers is available free on the App Store with an optional Premium subscription.
